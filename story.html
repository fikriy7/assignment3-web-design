Who we are

Welcome to Mehtravel – more than just a travel agency, we're a promise to transport you into a world of breathtaking natural beauty and captivating culture across Malaysia's most celebrated islands.

Established in 2025, Mehtravel was born from a deep love for every sea breeze, every stretch of pristine white sand and every stunning sunrise that graces our nation's archipelago.

We believe that each island possesses its own unique spirit, waiting to be explored and experienced and our mission is to be the bridge between you and those unforgettable moments.
